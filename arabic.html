Aimed At Beginners

Each student will start from the very basics, with each class having a balance of listening, speaking and comprehension of classical Arabic, from a variety of books.

 

Twice-Weekly Classes

Classes will be held after 'Ishaa, on a Saturday and a Tuesday, for 1½ hours, at JILC. Each term is expected to last ten weeks, with four terms in total.

 

Brothers & Sisters Welcome

Classes will be held in a large room made up of two smaller rooms. Brothers will be in one and sisters in the other, with both able to see and hear the teacher directly.

 

Comprehensive Student Support

Students will be encouraged and supported to study extensively at home, with PDF notes provided, and access to online classroom tools.
